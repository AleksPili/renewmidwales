What is Renew?

RENEW Mid-Wales is a project to deliver education regarding sustainable living via the restoration of a the [NAME] historical Victorian Garden in Radnorshire.

    About the Project

RENEW is a centre for delivery of educational courses in the context of restoring a Victorian garden. It is run by the project's founder David Burridge with the intention of becoming a vibrant social space in which visitors can learn holistic techniques for restoring green spaces to provide sustainable and edible produce in harmony with local ecology.

A note from the Project Co-ordinator David Burridge


Brief Biography about you David. (Sorry my memory is shit, but maybe something about your previous garden projects)

I started Renew with two goals in mind - To develop an educational center offering opportunities for the public to learn about sustainable living & ecological conservation. As well as rescue and restore the walled garden from years of neglect. The techniques employed to restore the garden are replicable, sustainable, and teachable. It is my goal to help volunteers to learn how to grow their own food, restore natural habits and build low impact improvements in the context safe space of this beautiful historical site.
